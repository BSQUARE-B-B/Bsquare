A transformation roadmap turns strategy into a sequence of executable steps. At CurveClear we start with discovery, then produce a prioritized roadmap with clear dependencies and milestones.

## From strategy to roadmap

1. **Discovery**: Understand current state, pain points, and goals.
2. **Gap analysis**: Identify what is missing (process, technology, data).
3. **Prioritization**: Order initiatives by impact and dependency.
4. **Phasing**: Define phases with deliverables and success criteria.
